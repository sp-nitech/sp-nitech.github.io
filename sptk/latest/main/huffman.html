class HuffmanCoding

Generate variable-length code codewords based on Huffman coding.

The input is the probabilites or frequencies of \(N\) events:

\[ \begin{array}{cccc} p(0), & p(1), & \ldots, & p(N-1), \end{array} \]
and the output is the corresponding codewords:
\[ \begin{array}{cccc} c(0), & c(1), & \ldots, & c(N-1), \end{array} \]
where \(c(n) \in \{0,1\}^{\ast}\).

The implementation is based on priority queue.
