Eigenvectors.

Examples

>>> x = diffsptk.nrand(10, 3)
>>> x.size()
torch.Size([10, 4])
>>> pca = diffsptk.PCA(3, 3)
>>> e, _, _ = pca(x)
>>> e
tensor([1.3465, 0.7497, 0.4447])
>>> y = pca.transform(x)
>>> y.size()
torch.Size([10, 3])
